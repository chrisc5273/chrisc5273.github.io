Website Evaluations

Stripe

Stripe’s marketing site showcases developer-focused products with a clean grid, generous whitespace, and clear calls-to-action.

  • Contrast: Strong type scale and whitespace create clear hierarchy; accent colors highlight CTAs.
  • Repetition: Consistent buttons, card patterns, and spacing rhythm reinforce familiarity.
  • Alignment: Tight grid; headlines, copy, and illustrations align to columns for a tidy edge.
  • Proximity: Related text and actions are grouped (cards/sections), making scanning effortless.

ARNGREN (Catalogue)

A dense, catalogue-style page that’s visually chaotic—useful as a case study of what to avoid.

  • Contrast: Many clashing colors and busy backgrounds reduce readability and visual focus.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent typefaces, image styles, and ad blocks break visual continuity.
  • Alignment: Elements land on different visual axes; table-style layout yields ragged edges.
  • Proximity: Products and promos crowd each other with minimal whitespace, hurting scannability.
